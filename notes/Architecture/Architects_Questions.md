# AI Architect's Questions

These questions are used repeatedly throughout this program to guide technical decisions, evaluate AI systems, and improve architectural thinking.

The goal is not to memorize answers.

The goal is to learn to ask better questions.

The best architects I've worked with don't necessarily know the most answers.

They consistently ask the best questions.
